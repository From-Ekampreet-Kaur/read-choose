Chapter 6: Before the Fall

Knock. Knock.

It was almost gentle. Too gentle.

Ishaan stood frozen, staring at the door. The laptop screen behind him still glowed with that cursed video—FindHer_Final—her eyes screaming through pixels.

Another knock. Then a voice: calm, low, cold.

“We know you remember now.”

Ishaan opened the door slowly. A man in a brown coat stood there, clean-shaven, no badge, no name. He held a clipboard as if this were an ordinary visit.

“You’ve been watching,” the man said, stepping inside without permission. “That’s dangerous.”

“Who are you?” Ishaan asked, backing away.

“I’m the reminder. Of what you buried.”

Then everything shattered.
