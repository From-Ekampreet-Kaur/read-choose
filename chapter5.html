Chapter 5: Truths That Burn

Kabir’s words haunted him. “I didn’t kill him… but I know who did.”

But now Kabir lay unconscious in the hospital. No visitors allowed. No explanations given. Ishaan’s mind spun as he plugged the flash drive into his laptop.

A folder popped open. “FinalProject_Truth” Inside—videos. Audio clips. Screenshots. Edited photos.

Arjun’s voice crackled through: “Ishaan’s memory is breaking. He doesn’t remember what we did in the woods last year. But I do.”

Ishaan’s heart thundered. What did he mean? A blurred image loaded next—three boys, hands bloodied, digging.

Names flickered in his head. Places. Moments. Things he knew... or thought he did.

Then a file named: “FindHer_Final” It was a video. Static. Then a girl’s face. Crying. Screaming.

Suddenly it hit him like a train: They had covered something up. Someone. Some night. And he had forgotten.

His reflection in the screen stared back, foreign. And then… the knock on the door.
