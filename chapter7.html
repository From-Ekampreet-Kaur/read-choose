Chapter 7: The Forgotten

The world was still. Ishaan stood in the middle of the campus courtyard, surrounded by people he’d known for years. But they looked at him like he was a stranger.

“Kabir?” he asked. A pause. A few confused glances. “Who?” someone whispered.

“Arjun,” he tried. “The boy who died.” A girl tilted her head. “I think you need help.”

His chest tightened. He ran to the admin office. Files — gone. No “Kabir Kapoor.” No “Arjun Mishra.” Yearbook photos — blank. Their faces replaced by static.

He found their dorm room. Bedsheets tucked perfectly. Two untouched beds. As if no one had ever slept there. “I’m losing my mind,” Ishaan whispered.
