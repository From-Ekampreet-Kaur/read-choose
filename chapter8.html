Chapter 8: The Archive Below

The knife. The photo. The red moon.

Ishaan had proof now. But what use was the truth when the world was blind to it?

He needed answers. He needed to know who had rewritten reality itself. So he started digging.

Through records. Through silence. Through lies. A janitor. A whisper. A locked door beneath the science block.

"The archive," they said. "No one goes down there anymore."

So Ishaan went.
